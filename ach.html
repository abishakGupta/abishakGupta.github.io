Previous Positions

  • University Postdoctoral Fellow, Center for Quantitative Medicine, University of Connecticut, CT, USA.
    Start date: July 2017, End date: July 2019.
    Included postdoctoral research on developing novel simulation and optimization algorithms for COPASI, a widely used simulator for biochemical processes.
  • Postdoctoral Researcher, Computational Systems Medicine, Institute of Molecular Medicine (FIMM), University of Helsinki, Helsinki, Finland.
    Start date: March 2016, End date: June 2017.
    Included postdoctoral research on developing models of cancer signaling pathways to better understand the multi-target inhibition effects.
  • Researcher (Doctoral Student), Laboratory of Biosystem Dynamics, Computational Systems Biology Research Group, Tampere University of Technology, Tampere, Finland.
    Start date: June 2012, End date: February 2016.
    Included postgraduate research focused on developing models of dynamic bacterial population to understand the biophysics of segregation of macromolecules in Escherichia coli.
  • Research Assistant, Laboratory of Biosystem Dynamics, Computational Systems Biology Research Group, Tampere University of Technology, Tampere, Finland.
    Start date: March 2011, End date: May 2012.
    Included M.Sc. research on understanding models of biological processes and Masters level course-works.
